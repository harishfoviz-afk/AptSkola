About This Summer Camp

The 21-Day Online Reading & Creativity Summer Camp is an enriching program designed to foster a love and habit for reading in children from grades 1 to 5. Through interactive online sessions, the program teaches foundational reading skills, creative writing, and expressive storytelling. It is tailored for parents seeking a meaningful summer learning program for kids that prevents summer learning loss. Children benefit from engaging activities like thinking games and story illustration, leading to improved communication skills, confidence, and a lifelong curiosity for knowledge.
